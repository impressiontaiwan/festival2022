About us

Taiwan Film Festival
Berlin

Taiwan Film Festival is the largest festival in Europe that offers a platform for Taiwan’s diverse film scene. The focus of the film festival is primarily on excellent conceptual and artistic films that provide unique perspectives on the Taiwanese and Asian arts. On event days, in addition to unique film series, we present multifacetedprograms, discussions and discussions with filmmakers and experts from the Far East. A continuous dialogue process will be accompanied by numerous film screenings and creative cultural exchange. Our goal is to create an intercultural bridge between Germany and the Taiwanese arts and to promote new visions of cross-cultural understanding in terms of Taiwan and Asia.

Surpport us

Our film festival is not only supported by the engagement of many film enthusiasts and the active collaborators in the association. As an official non-profit association, the success of our organization and our work comes from financial support through sponsorship. Only in this way it is possible to create and present a high-quality program every year.

The financial support is possible in several ways, such as through advertising in the program or in the promotional trailer from the film screenings. We would be glad to create tailor-made concepts according to your ideas in order to attract the greatest possible attention and to underline your active competitiveness.

We are also very grateful for the private donations and sponsorship, which is tax deductible in Germany. For more information and individual arrangements please contact us via impression.taiwan@gmail.com.

Taiwan Film Festival Berlin (TFFB) is one of the largest festivals in Europe that offers a vital platform for Taiwan’s diverse film scene. Founded in 2017, TFFB keeps focusing on excellent feature films and documentaries, as well as providing unique perspectives with multifaceted programs every year.
