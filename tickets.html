FAQs
How to buy the tickets for opening or closing screening/event?
Both screenings are cooperated with Friedrich-Naumann-Stiftung für die Freiheit. Admission is free and only need to registration on the website.
Do those films have subtitles?
All films will have English or German subtitles. For more information, please refer to the film descriptions.
Where can I buy the ticket?
You can purchase the ticket either online or at Kino in der Kulturbrauerei. Payment methods include credit card, Paypal, Sofort Überweisung, or Cash (only at cinema).
What is Vorverkaufsgebühr/ advance booking fee?
When you buy tickets via online ticket system, the prices of tickets from advance booking offices will carry a ca. 10% service fee. It is the service charge for the cinema.
Taiwan Film Festival Berlin (TFFB) is one of the largest festivals in Europe that offers a vital platform for Taiwan’s diverse film scene.
